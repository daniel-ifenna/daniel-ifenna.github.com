WELCOME TO MY WORLD OF DATA.

Hi, my name is Daniel Ifenna. I am a Nigerian and a newcomer to the world of data analytics. My journey into this field began during my undergraduate studies in International Relations. You might wonder why someone with a background in International Relations would transition to data analytics. The answer is simple: in my field, we are trained to be curious, to explore possibilities, and to create scenarios for events that may or may not happen. The primary "data" we use to create solutions or predict future events is history. In this way, International Relations and data analytics share a common foundation—analyzing information to uncover patterns and insights. However, the processes involved are quite different. Transitioning to data analytics has been challenging yet rewarding, and I found it surprisingly easy to adapt because of my curiosity and analytical mindset. I’m driven by a deep desire to solve global challenges using data-driven insights. For me, every dataset is an opportunity to learn, grow, and make a meaningful impact. I’m excited to continue this journey and contribute to shaping a better world through data.
