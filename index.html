About Me

To my ambitious entrepreneurs, growing businesses, and global-minded professionals, I see you. You're building bold ideas, navigating complex markets, and looking for clarity in a world of noise.

I’m Daniel Ifenna, a Global Business Adviser and Data Strategist with a passion for turning complexity into clarity and insight into growth. My analytics journey began at the National Institute of Information Technology (NIIT) Abuja, where I built a strong foundation in data interpretation and strategic thinking.

My professional edge was sharpened through advanced training with IBM, which deepened my ability to solve complex business problems and elevate decision-making with a data-informed lens.

As a proud graduate of Covenant University, and with a qualification in stakeholder management, I understand how to connect business goals with human priorities because meaningful progress happens when strategy, data, and people align.

Unlike generic consultants, I bring a story-first approach rooted in personal experience, enriched by business intelligence, and shaped by a commitment to real-world impact. Whether you’re scaling a local venture or navigating global markets, I help you move with clarity, purpose, and measurable results.

“Your business runs on what you know not just how much you hold.”

I envision a world where businesses of all sizes regardless of geography can access the tools, insights, and frameworks needed to thrive sustainably. My mission is to support the next generation of enterprises in building lasting impact, not just within their local contexts, but on the global stage.
